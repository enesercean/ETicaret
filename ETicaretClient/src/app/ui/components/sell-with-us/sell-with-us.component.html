Common Questions About Selling

You can sell almost any legal product on our platform - from handmade crafts to electronics, clothing, home goods, beauty products, and more. We have specific policies regarding certain restricted items, which you can review in our seller policy guide.

Our fee structure is straightforward and competitive:

  • No monthly subscription required to get started
  • A small listing fee of $0.20 per item
  • 5% transaction fee on the sale price (including shipping)
  • Payment processing fee of 3% + $0.25 per transaction

Volume discounts are available for high-volume sellers.

You have flexible options for shipping:

  • Ship items yourself using any carrier
  • Use our discounted shipping labels
  • Opt for our fulfillment service where we store and ship for you

For returns, you can set your own return policy, but we recommend offering at least a 30-day return window for the best customer experience.

We process payments every two weeks for all completed orders. Funds are directly deposited to your linked bank account. New sellers may have a 3-day hold period on their first few transactions as a security measure.

We provide comprehensive support to ensure your success:

  • Dedicated onboarding specialist for the first 30 days
  • 24/7 seller support via chat, email, and phone
  • Extensive knowledge base and video tutorials
  • Seller community forum for peer advice and networking
  • Monthly webinars on selling strategies and platform updates
